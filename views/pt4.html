Some of the FAQs answered by our doctors

Q. Is surgery the only option to release nerve trapped between discs?

A-Hi. This is a very common problem. Surgery is definitly a good option but not the best option. Treatment mainly depends on the symptoms and not only the mri report Studies have shown not much difference between surgical and non surgical treatment So what i would suggest is an initial non surgical treatment with analgesics. Muscle relaxants. Neuroprotectors and a low dose streoids to reduce the inflammation around the nerve.
